I CAN'T BREATHE

Season 1

"I Can't Breathe: Remembering George Floyd and the Call for Justice"
is a rallying cry for justice and equality, inspired by George Floyd's death in Minneapolis.
It calls for accountability and change against systemic racism and police brutality,
emphasizing the need to support marginalized communities
and address root causes of racial injustice.
